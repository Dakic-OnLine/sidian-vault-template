# Learn Spanish

Reach conversational fluency in Spanish within 12 months.

## Goal Statement

Be able to hold a 30-minute conversation with a native speaker on everyday topics without switching to English.

## Success Criteria

- Complete B2 level course
- Hold 30-minute conversation
- Watch and understand a Spanish film without subtitles
- Read a short novel in Spanish

## Timeline

- **Month 1-3**: Complete beginner course (A1-A2)
- **Month 4-6**: Intermediate grammar and vocabulary (B1)
- **Month 7-9**: Conversation practice with native speakers
- **Month 10-12**: Immersion through media and literature (B2)

## Motivation

Want to connect with Spanish-speaking friends and travel more confidently in Spanish-speaking countries.
