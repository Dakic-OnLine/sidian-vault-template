# Learn Spanish - Plan

Goal: [[Learn Spanish]]

## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)
- [ ] Choose and purchase beginner course (Duolingo/Babbel/Pimsleur)
- [ ] Complete 30 minutes of study daily
- [ ] Learn basic vocabulary (top 500 words)
- [ ] Master present tense conjugations
- [ ] Practice pronunciation with shadowing technique

## Phase 2: Building (Months 4-6)
- [ ] Start intermediate course
- [ ] Find language exchange partner
- [ ] Have 2 conversation sessions per week
- [ ] Learn past and future tenses
- [ ] Read graded readers (A2-B1 level)

## Phase 3: Immersion (Months 7-9)
- [ ] Watch Spanish content daily (Netflix/YouTube)
- [ ] Journal in Spanish 3x per week
- [ ] Take lessons with native speaker tutor
- [ ] Learn subjunctive mood and advanced grammar
- [ ] Expand vocabulary to 2000+ words

## Phase 4: Fluency (Months 10-12)
- [ ] Read first novel in Spanish
- [ ] Have week-long conversation challenge (Spanish only)
- [ ] Take DELE B2 exam (optional certification)
- [ ] Join Spanish-speaking community/group
- [ ] Plan trip to Spanish-speaking country

## Daily Habits
- [ ] Study 30 minutes minimum
- [ ] Review flashcards during commute
- [ ] Listen to Spanish podcast while exercising

## Weekly Checkpoints
- [ ] Friday: Review progress and adjust plan
- [ ] Sunday: Plan specific goals for next week
