# Quarterly Strategy - 2026 Q1

## Theme

Foundations: Build systems that support long-term growth

## Core Focus Areas

1. **Language Learning**: Establish Spanish as a daily habit
2. **Creative Output**: Complete first major writing project
3. **Physical Health**: Rebuild consistent exercise routine

## Goals Created

- [[Learn Spanish]] - 12-month journey to conversational fluency
- [[Complete writing project]] - Finish 6-chapter draft
- [[Exercise regularly]] - 4x per week minimum

## Monthly Breakdown

### January
- Spanish: Complete beginner module (A1 level)
- Writing: Draft 4 chapters
- Exercise: Test different routines, find what sticks

### February
- Spanish: Reach A2 level, start conversation practice
- Writing: Complete remaining chapters, begin editing
- Exercise: Establish morning routine, 16 sessions/month

### March
- Spanish: Hold first conversation with native speaker
- Writing: Complete first draft full edit
- Exercise: Maintain consistency, add variety

## Success Metrics

- Spanish: 90-day study streak, B1 vocabulary
- Writing: Complete edited manuscript
- Exercise: 48+ sessions in quarter

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Burnout from too many goals | Focus on 2 at a time, rotate focus monthly |
| Loss of motivation | Weekly reviews, track streaks, celebrate small wins |
| Unexpected interruptions | Build buffer time into schedule, be flexible |
