# Example: Deep Work Concept

Thinking about how deep work applies to personal productivity.

## Core Idea

Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task.

## Applications

- **Writing**: dedicate 2-hour blocks for focused writing
- **Coding**: eliminate notifications during development sessions
- **Learning**: study complex topics without interruption

## Thoughts

The challenge is protecting deep work time from shallow commitments (email, meetings, admin).
